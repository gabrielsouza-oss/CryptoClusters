Platform

Crypto Clusters is an analytical platform created to foment the cryptocurrencies market with relevant analysis, based on data-science.

We want to deliver a different experience to our users, using Statistical Methods combined with Unsupervised Machine Learning.

The main objective of our analysis is to show the affinity of cryptocurrencies between each other and find groups of cryptos with shared attributes.

What is Clustering?

Clustering is the task of grouping a set of objects in such a way that objects in the same group (called a cluster) are more similar to each other than to those in other groups (clusters).

All features used on Crypto Clusters analysis are the most common market data from top 100 Cryptocurrencies, such as: MarketCap, Volume (24h), Max Supply, Circulating Supply and Circulating Rate.
