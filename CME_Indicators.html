What is CME Futures Contracts?

The CME is the Chicago Mercantile Exchange which provides indices (prices) for all sort of commodities

The BTC Future Contract was introduced in late 2017 and has been mostly traded by institutional investors.

Bitcoin futures allow investors to gain exposure to Bitcoin without having to hold the underlying cryptocurrency. Like a futures contract for a commodity or stock index, Bitcoin futures allow investors to speculate on the future price of Bitcoin

What does Change Potential means?

The Change Potential Represents the percentage of upside or downside needed to daily Bitcoin Price reach the Future Contracts Predicted price.

Historically, the CME Gaps has been filled 95% of time and could be a great indicator for market changes.
